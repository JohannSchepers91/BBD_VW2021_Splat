Easy puzzle solution:

Repeat
walk
if IsTileAhead(Wall) OR IsTileAhead(Gate) do turn(right)
Until IsColor(Red)

Turn(around)

repeat
walk
if IsTileCurrent(Junction) or IsTileAhead(Wall) do Turn(right)
until IsColor(Blue)

turn(around)

if IsTileAhead(wall) do turn(left)
until Yellow

until IsTileCurrent(Junction)

Walk
until IsTileCurrent(Junction)

if IsTileCurrent(Junction) or if IsTileAhead(Wall) do turn(right)
